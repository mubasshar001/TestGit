Client: Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs

Consultant: Nizar Kurdi

Project description:

This section extends over a length of 50Km and contains 24 bridges and interchanges most of them spanning deep in the valleys. The scope of work was the design of precast girders, procurement of moulds and technical know- how of introducing the precast segmental system plus the complete prestressing work and the erection of precast girders over the deep valleys for which the special technique of launching beams was introduced.

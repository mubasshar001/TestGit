Client: Adeem Al Wataniah for Construction and Development

Consultant: Saudi Diyar

Project description:

This project located on the Jeddah Corniche currently the highest tower in Jeddah consists of the West Tower that consists of 56 floors rising 250m from the ground level and an East Tower consisting of 17 floors and a central podium consisting of 8 floors for parking space for a total built up area of 165,000m².

The building will be used as office space with state of the art smart systems pre-installed.
